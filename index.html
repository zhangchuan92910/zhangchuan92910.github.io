About Me

I am a Master's student at the University of Victoria, specializing in Graph Theory. My current research, under the supervision of Dr. Jing Huang, focuses on exploring structural characterizations of digraph classes, particularly strongly chordal and semicomplete digraphs.

I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Simon Fraser University and a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. This interdisciplinary background gives me a unique perspective, blending theoretical computer science with practical engineering and software development skills.

Previously, I worked as a Product Engineer at Roboterra Inc., where I designed robotics curricula and developed drivers for educational robots. I'm also an active member of the competitive programming community and enjoy tackling complex algorithmic problems.

When I'm out of my academic work, I enjoy trying delicious foods, playing video games (now making my own instead of play), and archery (starting from 2025)...
